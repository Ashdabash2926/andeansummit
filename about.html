About

Twelve years
in the mountains.

Andean Summit is a Huaraz-based travel and adventure agency operating in the Cordillera Blanca and Huayhuash since 2012.

Mission

Safety, reliability,
comfort.

Our main goal is to provide maximum safety, reliability and comfort to our customers, so that they can enjoy the different activities offered in Peru. We strive to offer customers an unforgettable experience on each tour, trek, and climb.

Vision

World-class Peruvian
tourism.

To reach world-class status in Peruvian tourism while staying innovative in the experiences we offer and preserving local traditions. The satisfaction of our clients is the highest honour.

Team

Local guides,
cooks and porters.

Andean Summit employs experienced cooks and porters alongside a dedicated reservations and logistics team. Every guide is fluent in English and Spanish, with French available on request.
